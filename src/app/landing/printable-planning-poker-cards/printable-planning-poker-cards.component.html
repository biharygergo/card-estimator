How to Use Printed Cards?

1

Download free PDF

You can just click download above and save the high-quality PDF file to your computer.

2

Print the cards

Print the cards on A4 paper. To get the best quality print, we recommend printing on thicker paper. You can do this at home or a local print shop.

3

Laminate the cards

Laminate the cards to protect them from wear and tear. Laminating will make the cards feel more durable and easier to use.

4

Cut the cards

Cut the cards along the cutting guides. For rounded corners, you can use a special corner cutter or a rounded corner punch.

5

Distribute the cards

Distribute the cards to your team members. Everyone should have their own set.

6

Estimate and Discuss

When the team is meeting in person, use the cards to estimate and discuss. When the team is remote, use PlanningPoker.live to estimate and discuss.

Planning Poker is now available within Zoom!

Use the Zoom integration to easily share planning poker sessions with your team.
You don't even need to leave your Zoom meeting to have the same great planning experience as before.

download Install in Zoom

How does it work?

To add the app to your Zoom account, click the "Install" button above or install the app from Zoom Marketplace. You'll need to grant basic permissions for the app to appear inside your meeting.

Once the app is installed, you can open it from the "Apps" menu in Zoom. The app will appear in the side-panel of your meeting.

After you've created a planning poker session, just click "Invite others" and a personalized invitation will be sent to everyone in the meeting.

Once you are all in the same meeting and the app is open for everyone, you can start planning your project. Set a topic by clicking "Edit" next to "Topic of Round #1" and decide which card set you want to use.

Each member votes on their own, and once all votes are cast the leader presses "Reveal Votes" to reveal all votes. To start the next round, click "New Round" (or "Next Round", if you've set the topics beforehand from the sidebar) The team continues this process until all topics have been voted on.

Uninstalling the App

If you wish to uninstall the App, go to the installed apps section on Zoom Marketplace and click Uninstall next to "Planning Poker".

Getting Support and Reporting Bugs

Are you stuck on a question or issue with the app? Check out the Frequently Asked Questions, maybe your query already has an answer.

If you need additional support or have found a bug, please open a ticket here on GitHub and our developers will try to help as fast as possible. We try respond to tickets within 24 hours. If you need immediate assistance, please contact us at hello@planningpoker.live.

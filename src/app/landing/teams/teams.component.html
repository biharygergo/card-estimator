How does it work?

Check out the main steps to installing the app below or learn more in our knowledge base article for setting up PlanningPoker.live in Teams.

To add the app to your Teams account, open AppSource and click the "Get it now" button. You might need to ask your Workspace administrator to whitelist the app for use.

Once the app is installed, you need to add it to a meeting. This should be the meeting for your regular planning session. The app will be installed as a "Tab" in your meeting window.

You can also add the app to a channel or a chat. It will appear as a permanent tab in this scenario.

After you've created a planning poker session, just click "Invite" and the link to join the current session will be shared. You can also share the app to the meeting stage via the "Share to Stage" button on the top of the app sidebar.

Once you are all in the same meeting and the app is open for everyone, you can start planning your project. Set a topic by clicking "Edit topic" next to "Topic of Round #1" and decide which card set you want to use.

Each member votes on their own, and once all votes are cast the leader presses "Reveal Votes" to reveal all votes. To start the next round, click "New Round" (or "Next Round", if you've set the topics beforehand from the sidebar) The team continues this process until all topics have been voted on.

Getting Support and Reporting Bugs

Are you stuck on a question or issue with the app? Check out the Frequently Asked Questions, maybe your query already has an answer.

If you need additional support or have found a bug, please open a ticket here on GitHub and our developers will try to help as fast as possible. We try respond to tickets within 24 hours. If you need immediate assistance, please contact us at hello@planningpoker.live.

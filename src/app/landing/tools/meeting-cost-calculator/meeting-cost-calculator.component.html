Why Calculate Meeting Costs?

Improve Meeting Efficiency

Understanding the cost helps teams make informed decisions about meeting length, frequency, and attendee lists. When you can quantify the investment, you're more likely to run focused, productive meetings.

Optimize Agile Ceremonies

Sprint planning, daily stand-ups, and retrospectives are valuable—but keeping them efficient saves time and money. Use this calculator to evaluate your agile ceremonies and find opportunities for improvement.

Better Time Management

Visualizing costs encourages async communication when appropriate and focused, productive meetings when necessary. Not every discussion needs a meeting—sometimes Slack or email works just as well.
